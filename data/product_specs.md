# WidgetPro 3000 Specifications

## Power
- Voltage: 110-240V AC
- Power consumption: 45 watts (active), 2 watts (standby)
- Battery backup: 4 hours at full load

## Physical
- Dimensions: 12 x 8 x 3 inches
- Weight: 2.4 pounds
- Color: Matte black or pearl white

## Connectivity
- WiFi: 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac (dual-band 2.4GHz and 5GHz)
- Bluetooth: 5.2
- USB: Two USB-C ports (one supports 65W PD charging)
- Ethernet: 1 Gbps RJ-45

## Warranty
The WidgetPro 3000 includes a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing
defects. Extended warranties of 3 or 5 years are available at checkout.
