ACME Corp Refund Policy

Premium subscribers may cancel within 14 days of purchase for a full refund.
After 14 days, refunds are prorated based on the number of days used in the
current billing cycle. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and
returned to the original payment method.

Annual plans purchased during promotional sales are non-refundable after
30 days. Enterprise customers must contact their account manager to request
a refund and are subject to the terms of their signed agreement.

Refund requests must be submitted through the customer portal at
support.acme.com/refunds. Refunds cannot be issued for partial months.
