Shipping FAQ

Q: How long does standard shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the continental US.
Express shipping takes 2-3 business days and costs an additional $15.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to 47 countries. International orders take 10-21 business
days and customers are responsible for any customs duties.

Q: What is the cutoff time for same-day processing?
A: Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on weekdays will be processed the same
day. Weekend orders are processed on the next business day.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
A: Address changes are possible within 1 hour of placing the order by
contacting support@acme.com.
